University of Florida — B.S. Mechanical Engineering · GPA 3.97

I recently earned my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Florida. Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have developed a strong foundation for process optimization, mechanical design, and leading team projects. My industry experience includes a role as a Manufacturing Performance Engineer Intern at PIM Brands, where I improved packaging line efficiency and applied continuous improvement methodologies to the mass production of Welch's Fruit Snacks.

At UF I led a team of nine engineers to design and prototype a tether deployment system for two 12U satellites, manufactured and tested an oscillating air engine, completed finite element analyses, developed a machine learning model for sea turtle conservation, and served as an FEA Teaching Assistant. I am a member of Tau Beta Pi Honor Society (top 1/8 of class) and am passionate about pursuing a career in the defense industry to apply my technical skills toward the protection of our country.
